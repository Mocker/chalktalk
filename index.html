Chalktalk's interface consists primarily of two conventions:
Clicking and dragging on the background draws a set of lines, and
By clicking without dragging, you can activate a series of invisible radial menus.

The reason they're invisible is because of Chalktalk's use in presentations: the focus should be on the content you're showing instead of the interface. However, as a helpful tool for beginners, pressing Space activates "help mode", which makes these radial menus visible. You may also press the UI button at the top right.
